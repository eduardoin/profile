My favorite Books

The Centaur in the Garden, by Moacyr Scliar

Guedali Tartakovsky celebrates his 38th birthday. It is only now that Guedali is able to revel in memories of glorious times past. Born a centaur, Guedali describes his family's flight from Russia to Brazil at the turn of the century, the shock of his birth, the loving care of his parents and his sisters, the mounting resentment of his brother, and his extraordinary experiences being raised as a Jew. He meets his life companion--a centauress. Together they embark on a journey to create a place where the human and the wild can live in peaceful coexistence.
